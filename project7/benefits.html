5 Benefits of Music on Everyday life

-Benefits on the Heart: Research has concluded that music has many benefits on heart health. Research has concluded that while music is playing, blood flows easier, as well as it can reduce stress, heart rate, lower blood pressure, and increase serotonin/endorphin levels in the blood.

-Reduction of Stress: Scientists have found that when music is played, biomedical stress relievers are released in the brain.

-Facilitation of Memories: Music can be very influential in different parts of ones life. Certain songs can correlate with different eras and when played, it can facilitate/remind people of the times in their life where the songs remained prevalent. Even in Alzheimers patients going through Music Therapy, music can unlock memories from their life.
